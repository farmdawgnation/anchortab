Anchor Tab can help you Connect with your Followers

When you embed the Anchor Tab on your website, you can expand your influence by collecting email addresses from your visitors.

How it Works

Create

After signing up, gain access to the Anchor Tab editor and begin creating your first Anchor Tab. Customize everything including the colors, text and even a unique image. Use the image to promote a product, service, e-book, your brand, and much more. If you use an email marketing service, connect it to the Anchor Tab and you’re ready to put it on your site.
