Anchor Tab can help you Connect with your Followers

Anchor Tab is an elegant bar that you can embed on any site to engage your visitors and collect their email information. Start building your tribe and expanding your influence.

How it Works

< >

Create

After signing up, gain access to the Anchor Tab editor and begin creating your first Anchor Tab. Customize everything including the colors, text and more. If you use an email marketing service, connect it to the Anchor Tab. Otherwise, we'll store all of your records on our site for you to access at anytime. Now, you're ready to put it on your site.

Embed

Now that you’ve created your Anchor Tab, you’re ready to embed it on your website. Simply click the “Embed” button and a code snippet will be generated for you to embed on your site. Copy and paste that code into the <body> section of your editor and you’re done! Once you do this, you’ll never have to do it again. Your tab can be edited from the member section of this website and all changes will be added automatically to the tab on your site. It’s that easy.

Collect

Once you've embedded the Anchor Tab on your site, it does the magic for you. Every visitor will see the Anchor Tab when they visit your site and have the opportunity to give you their email information. We'll keep track of every email address you collect, even if you don't use an email marketing service, and you'll be able to export a CSV file of your subscribers at any time.

Expand Your Influence

At its core, Anchor Tab is all about one thing: helping you expand your influence. By collecting your visitor's email information, you can continue to stay connected and cultivate relationships with your tribe. Ready to get started?

Features

  • Email Collection

    Collect email addresses from visitors and add them to your list to stay connected.

  • Customization

    Choose a color scheme that matches your brand and custom text to promote your company or product.

  • Multi-Platform

    We support any platform that allows you to paste in embed code. Wordpress, Joomla, Blogger, Typepad, Tumblr, and more!

  • Multiple Tabs

    Create multiple tabs to embed on different sites or create custom tabs to promote a seasonal special offer and keep your standard tab on-ice so you don't have to recreate it.

Works with and without an email marketing service.

If you currently use a supported email marketing service (ConstantContact or MailChimp), you can connect your Anchor Tab account to it. Otherwise, we store all the addresses for you to access anytime.
