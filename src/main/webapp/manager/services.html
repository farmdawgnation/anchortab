Connected Services

Anchor Tab is capable of pumping submissions of emails to the 3rd party service of your choice. To send incoming emails to a third party service, connect to your service below and follow the steps to authenticate Anchor Tab to access your account. Then, go edit the tab you want emails recorded from. Select the service and the list from that service that you'd like to use.

Need to use a service that's not available? Let us know!
