About Us

Anchor Tab is a joint venture between Crazy Goat Creative, a boutique consulting and product company, area, and Cirion Group, a business consulting firm. We're all individuals passionate about what we're doing, and with strong ideals that are continually influencing the direction we take with Anchor Tab. Success or failure we have decided that from the start this business will be open, it will be fun, and it will be human.

We're open because we have benefited from open technology and give back to those communities that have helped us get where we are. We're fun because life is too short to take yourself too seriously, and we're human in how we deal with our customers - both on days when things are going well and on days they aren't. So, if you'd like to get to know the team, read on.
